Guru Purnima
April 5, 2025 7:00 AM onwards

Special celebrations for Guru Purnima with Guru Puja, Sahasranama, and discourse on the importance of Guru. The schedule includes:

  • Guru Paduka Pooja
  • Special abhishekam to Sri Raghavendra Swami
  • Discourse by eminent scholars
  • Distribution of prasadam
  • Cultural program in the evening

All devotees are welcome to participate in this auspicious occasion.

Hanuman Jayanti
April 12, 2025 5:30 AM onwards

Celebrate the birth of Lord Hanuman with special rituals and programs. The celebration will include:

  • Special abhishekam to Lord Hanuman
  • Recitation of Hanuman Chalisa (108 times)
  • Sindoor offering
  • Discourse on the significance of Hanuman Jayanti
  • Special prasadam distribution

Devotees are encouraged to wear orange/red attire for the celebrations.

Aradhana Mahotsava
March 18-20, 2025 | 6:00 AM onwards

Join us for the grand annual Aradhana Mahotsava celebrations commemorating Sri Raghavendra Swami. This three-day event is filled with special rituals, cultural programs, and spiritual discourses.

Detailed Schedule:
Day Time Event
Day 1 (March 18) 6:00 AM Suprabhatam & Special Abhishekam
8:00 AM Sahasranama Archana
11:00 AM Maha Mangalarati & Prasada Vitarana
4:00 PM Discourse by Scholar
7:00 PM Cultural Program
Day 2 (March 19) 6:00 AM Suprabhatam & Panchamrita Abhishekam
8:00 AM Rudra Homa
11:00 AM Maha Mangalarati & Prasada Vitarana
4:00 PM Vedic Chanting
7:00 PM Bhajans & Cultural Program
Day 3 (March 20) 6:00 AM Suprabhatam & Maha Abhishekam
8:00 AM Ganapati Homa
11:00 AM Maha Mangalarati & Prasada Vitarana
4:00 PM Valedictory Function
7:00 PM Procession & Cultural Program
Special Arrangements:
  • Accommodation for outstation devotees (limited availability, please register in advance)
  • Annadana (free meals) for all devotees throughout the event
  • Special seating for elderly and differently-abled devotees
  • Live streaming of main events on our social media channels
How to Register:

Please fill out the registration form at the temple office or online through our website to confirm your participation, especially if you require accommodation.
