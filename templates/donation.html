How Your Donations Help

Annadhanam

Your donations help feed devotees and the needy who visit the Math daily. We provide free meals to hundreds of people every day.

Vedic Education

Support our Vedic pathashala where students learn ancient Vedic knowledge, Sanskrit, and traditional rituals.

Community Services

We conduct free medical camps, provide educational scholarships, and support underprivileged families in the community.

Temple Maintenance

Helps maintain the temple premises, conduct daily poojas, special events, and festivals according to tradition.
